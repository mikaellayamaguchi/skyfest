DID YOU KNOW?

The Philippines has become a must-visit destination for adventurers, thanks to the Philippine International Hot Air Balloon Fiesta, which has been showcasing hot air balloons and other aircraft since 1994.

FAQs
  • What are the usual festival hours?
    • 5:10 AM (Sunrise) and 5:30 PM (Sunset)
  • Is it safe to ride in a hot air balloon?
    • Yes, riding in a hot air balloon is generally considered safe. Hot air balloons are stable, fly in calm conditions, are piloted by experienced professionals, and undergo regular safety checks.
  • What other activities are there besides balloon rides?
    • Food vendors: Enjoy a wide selection of delicious food and drinks from local vendors.
    • Children's activities: Many festivals offer special activities for children, such as face painting, balloon animals, etc.
    • Park: Relax and enjoy the scenic surroundings in a designated park area.
